About Us

Overview

A system that provides a solution for organizing parent patrols.

we provide up to date date so you can understand where you can volunteer and join patrols that relevant for you

If you want to join the patrol management you can send an request to the admin now, you can add a personal text to your application and you can join the team of patrol managers and lead tours yourself

As a patrol manager you will be able to get data on crime in the city, and you will know where it is most relevant to conduct patrols

Send request to admin now!
Join to patrol in the city

On the tours page you can see all the existing tours in Be'er Sheva, sort by priority, hours, areas and need and you can join the tour.

In addition as a patrol manager you can manage your tours and read the tour summaries from the tour page

Emergency!

In an emergency you can call the phone numbers of any of the patrol managers for help. You can also find more useful phone numbers on this page

Your page

On your page you can see all the patrols you have registered for and all the details about each patrol
